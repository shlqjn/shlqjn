Because Every Child Deserves Both Parents

Parental alienation is a form of emotional abuse where one parent manipulates a child into rejecting the other parent.
It creates confusion, pain, and emotional harm that can last a lifetime.

This page was created to raise awareness, offer support, and share tools that can help families navigate and heal from this experience.
